About Air Quality Tracker

Air Quality Tracker is an advanced tool designed to provide real-time air quality information for cities and locations worldwide. By monitoring key air quality indicators, the platform empowers individuals, businesses, and governments to make informed decisions to improve environmental health.

Key Metrics Tracked

  • Air Quality Index (AQI): A standardized measure used to describe air quality based on pollutant levels. It categorizes air quality from good to hazardous, making it easy to understand environmental conditions.
  • PM2.5: Fine particulate matter less than 2.5 micrometers in diameter. These particles can penetrate deep into the respiratory system and have significant health impacts.
  • PM10: Particulate matter with a diameter of less than 10 micrometers. While larger than PM2.5, these particles can still affect the respiratory system.
  • Ozone (O₃): A gas formed in the atmosphere, ozone plays a dual role—beneficial in the upper atmosphere, but harmful near the ground, where it can exacerbate respiratory issues.
